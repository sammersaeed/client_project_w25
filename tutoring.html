Tutoring Resources

The University of Michigan School of Information has an Academic Success Team dediated to supporting students inside and outside of the classroom. They work to connect students with tutors and further acedemic resources. There are two types of Peer Tutoring available to students, Programming Peer Tutoring and Math Peer Tutoring. Tutoring covers specific classes in the UMSI program and UMSI MADS program.


Programming Tutoring

Available for the following classes...

  • SI 106

    Programs, Information, and People

  • SI 206

    Data-Oriented Programming

  • SI 506

    Programming I

  • SI 507

    Intermediate Programming

  • IADS 505

    Data Manipulation (MADS)

  • General Python

    related course support (MADS)

Math Tutoring

Available for the following classes...

  • Linear Algebra

    (Available for MADS as well)

  • Statistics

    (Available for MADS as well)

  • General Math

    related course support (Available for MADS as well)

  • SIADS 502

    Math Methods I (MADS)

Some tutors provide tutoring for other classes, which can be seen under their biographies. Please see the Programming Peer Tutoring Site or the Math Peer Tutoring Site for more information on how to sign up.

Additional Resources

  • English Language Institute
  • LS&A Math Lab
